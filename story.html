Humble Beginnings

brownie.ind was born in 2019 from a home kitchen with a simple goal: to create fudgy brownies that could be everyone's favorite treat. Initially sold to friends and family, it expanded online with its main product, the brownie box, perfect for gifts or personal snacks.

Growth and Challenges

In 2020, brownie.ind opened a physical store in Malang, serving brownies and coffee in one place. Although the shop only lasted a year due to the pandemic, the spirit of growth never faded.

Our Journey Today

brownie.ind then launched the "Brownie Slice," a practical variation ideal for partnering with coffee shops. This product paved the way for distribution to various coffee shops in Malang and the surrounding areas. After a brief hiatus in 2022, distribution resumed with both old and new partners, even expanding to menu collaborations with an ice cream business. To this day, brownie.ind continues to grow as a local Malang brand that prioritizes homemade quality and authentic taste.
